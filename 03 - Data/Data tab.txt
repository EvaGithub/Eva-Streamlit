What are we looking at

	1: Textual Data
		Item description provided by seller. Has no standardization and is cluttered by links, hashtags an markup language.

	2: Image Data
		Item pictures provided by seller as 500x500 RGB images on white background.

	3: Classes
		There are 27 classes spanning a wide variety of items, from books to Gardening tools.

	4: Brief Data Analysis
		Unbalanced
		Missing textual data
		Repeated textual data
		Repeated images (same class)
		Placeholder images (repeats across classes)
